About

What is Squad Match?

Welcome to Squad Match, your ultimate companion in the world of local football! Our mission is to bridge the gap between football enthusiasts and the games that happen right in their neighborhood. Whether you're looking to join a match, find players to complete your squad, or simply discover where the next game is happening, Squad Match has got you covered.

At Squad Match, we believe that football is more than just a game; it's a community. That's why we've created a platform that not only makes it easy to find local matches but also connects you with fellow football lovers in your area. From casual kickabouts to competitive league games, our website is designed to cater to all levels of skill and commitment.

How it works:

Step 1. Create an account.
Step 2. Customize your profile.
Step 3. Visit Matches page to create or join a lobby.
Step 4. Confirm that you are in and wait for other players to join.
Step 4. Finally, the match should be scheduled. Prepare to show the world how good you are on the pitch!
